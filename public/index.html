Ping Yao
  Introduction
Pingyao (Simplified Chinese: 平遥; Traditional Chinese: 平遙) is an old county located in northen central China. Its history dates back some 2,700 years. During the Qing Dynasty (1644 - 1911), it was a financial center of China. Now it is renowned for the well-preserved city walls and other architectural styles. Pingyao is a World Heritage Site.
